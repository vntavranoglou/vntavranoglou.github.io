AI Governance Risk Assessment — ChatGPT Use in Companies

1. Overview

Many companies are starting to use AI tools like ChatGPT to improve efficiency. However, the use of AI introduces legal, operational, and reputational risks that must be managed.

2. Risks

  • Hallucinations: AI may generate incorrect or misleading information.
  • Data leakage: Employees may input confidential company data into AI tools.
  • Lack of accountability: It may be unclear who is responsible for AI-generated outputs.

3. Legal Risks

  • Data protection breaches (GDPR)
  • Incorrect legal advice
  • Intellectual property issues

4. Mitigation Measures

  • Require human review of all AI outputs
  • Prohibit input of confidential data
  • Train employees on AI risks
  • Implement internal AI usage policies

5. Conclusion

AI tools like ChatGPT can be valuable, but companies must implement governance measures to manage associated risks.
